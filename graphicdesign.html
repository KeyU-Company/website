Graphic Design Mastery Course

About this Course

Unlock Your Creative Potential and Launch Your Career in Graphic Design! Are you passionate about visual storytelling and eager to dive into the dynamic world of graphic design? Our "Graphic Design Fundamentals for Beginners" course is the perfect starting point for your creative journey. Whether you dream of becoming a professional graphic designer or aspire to enhance your design skills, this course is designed to empower you with the fundamental knowledge and hands-on experience you need.

What You'll Learn:

  • Design Essentials:Master the essential design principles, including layout, typography, color theory, and visual hierarchy, to create impactful and visually compelling designs.
  • Design Essentials:** Master the essential design principles, including layout, typography, color theory, and visual hierarchy, to create impactful and visually compelling designs.
  • Industry-Standard Tools:** Gain proficiency in industry-standard graphic design software, allowing you to transform your creative ideas into professionally crafted visual assets.
  • Practical Projects: Apply your newly acquired skills through a series of practical design projects, which will help you build a portfolio that showcases your creativity and expertise.
  • Expert Guidance:** Learn from experienced instructor who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and insights, and join a community of like-minded learners to foster collaboration and support.

Course Module

  • Module 1: Introduction to Graphic Design
  • Module 2: Graphic Design Fundamentals
  • Module 3: Design Softwares (Part One - Adobe Illustrator)
  • Module 4: Design Softwares (Part Two - Adobe Photoshop)
  • Module 5: Design Softwares (Part Three - Adobe Indesign)
  • Module 6: Finding your Niche + Classwork
  • Module 7: Building your Portfolio & Resume + Classwork
  • Module 8: Final Project
